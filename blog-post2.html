5 Mistakes to Avoid When Renting

  1. Not Researching – Always research the area, property, and landlord.
  2. Overlooking Damages – Inspect and document existing damage before moving in.
  3. Ignoring the Lease – Read the lease terms carefully before signing.
  4. Forgetting Renters Insurance – It protects your belongings from theft or damage.
  5. Mishandling Finances – Don’t overextend your budget; pay rent on time.
